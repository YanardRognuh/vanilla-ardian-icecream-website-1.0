About Us

Welcome to Ardian's Ice Cream Shop, the place where you can enjoy the most delicious and refreshing ice cream in the world. We are a family-owned business that started in 2020 with a passion for making ice cream from scratch using natural ingredients and local flavors. Our ice cream is made fresh every day in small batches, so you can always taste the difference. Whether you prefer classic flavors like vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry, or want to try something new like durian, mango, or coconut, we have something for everyone. Come and visit us today and treat yourself to a scoop of happiness!

Ice Creams

Scoop It Up

This is a fun and colorful ice cream menu that features different types of scoops, cones, toppings, and sauces. You can mix and match your favorite flavors and create your own masterpiece. Some of the flavors are Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, Mint, Cookie Dough, and Rainbow Sherbet. Some of the toppings are Sprinkles, Nuts, Chocolate Chips, Marshmallows, and Gummy Bears. Some of the sauces are Caramel, Chocolate, Strawberry, and Butterscotch.

Frosty Treats

This is a classic and elegant ice cream menu that showcases different kinds of frozen desserts, such as ice cream, gelato, sorbet, frozen yogurt, and soft serve. You can choose from various sizes, cups, or waffle cones. Some of the desserts are Chocolate Fudge Brownie, Pistachio Almond, Lemon Sorbet, Mango Frozen Yogurt, and Vanilla Soft Serve.

Ice Cream Factory

Ice cream factory is a unique and fun way to enjoy ice cream. You can choose your favorite flavor and toppings, and then add liquid nitrogen to freeze it yourself. Liquid nitrogen is a very cold substance that makes the ice cream smooth and creamy in seconds. It also creates a cool smoke effect that looks like magic. Come and experience the ice cream factory today!
